Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center. Here you can find guidance on how to use the ERP system.

1. Managing Products

Go to the Products page to add, update, or remove products from your inventory. You can track stock levels and product details easily.

2. Managing Suppliers

Use the Suppliers section to store supplier details including name, contact information, and purchase history.

3. Creating Purchase Invoices

Navigate to the Purchase Invoice page to record purchases made from suppliers. You can add items, quantities, and calculate totals.

4. Viewing Reports

Reports help you analyze sales performance, stock levels, and business activity.

5. Notifications

Notifications alert you about important updates like low stock items or pending orders.

If you still need assistance, please contact our support team.
